Our product

Sling is a free communication and shift scheduling software for restaurants and retail stores. Built around four features - newsfeed, messages, shifts and tasks, which makes it possible for managers to organize all aspects of their work on a single platform.

Sling is available on the web and on iOS and Android, making work accessible anywhere.
